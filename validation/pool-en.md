# Initial Survey — Reliqia (English)
> Google Forms format | Quick screening — 3 to 5 minutes

---

## FORM SETTINGS

**Title:** Collectors: 5 quick questions
**Description:** We're building something for collectors and want to understand how you operate today. Just 5 questions, takes less than 5 minutes. Fully anonymous.

---

## SECTION 1 — YOUR COLLECTION

**Question 1**
What do you collect?
- Type: Checkboxes (can select more than one)
- Options:
  - Vinyl records
  - CDs
  - Cassettes
  - Trading cards (Magic, Pokémon, etc.)
  - Concert shirts / memorabilia
  - Toys / figures
  - Other (specify)
- Required: Yes

---

**Question 2**
How often do you buy, sell, or trade items from your collection?
- Type: Multiple choice
- Options:
  - Several times a week
  - Once or twice a month
  - A few times a year
  - Rarely or never
- Required: Yes

---

## SECTION 2 — YOUR EXPERIENCE WITH PLATFORMS

**Question 3**
Which platforms do you use to buy or sell? (you can choose more than one)
- Type: Checkboxes
- Options:
  - Discogs
  - eBay
  - Facebook Marketplace / Facebook groups
  - MercadoLibre
  - Wallapop / Vinted
  - WhatsApp or Telegram groups
  - I don't use any platform
  - Other (specify)
- Required: Yes

---

**Question 4**
What is the biggest problem you have with the platforms you use today? (pick the one that resonates most)
- Type: Multiple choice
- Options:
  - Notifications arrive too late or are useless
  - There's no easy way to trade items directly with another collector
  - I don't know if the price I set (or pay) is right
  - I can't verify if what I'm buying is authentic
  - Fees are too high
  - I don't trust sellers / buyers
  - None of these apply to me
- Required: Yes

---

## SECTION 3 — NEW IDEAS

**Question 5**
How much value would you give to each of these features? (1 = no value, 5 = essential)

*Use the rating grid — one row per feature*

- Type: Multiple choice grid
- Rows:
  - Direct trade with another collector without selling and rebuying separately
  - Instant alert when exactly what you're looking for is listed, with full context
  - Authenticity score per item based on real photos + community expert validation
  - Public profile with your collection, reputation, and trade history
  - Automatic valuation before listing based on real historical prices
- Columns: 1 — 2 — 3 — 4 — 5
- Required: Yes

---

## SECTION 4 — CLOSING

**Question 6**
Would you be willing to participate in a 30-minute interview to tell us more about your experience? (can be by video call or written)
- Type: Multiple choice
- Options:
  - Yes, happy to
  - Maybe, depending on timing
  - Not right now
- Required: Yes

---

**Question 7** *(only if answered Yes or Maybe in question 6)*
If you'd like us to reach out, leave your email or Instagram handle.
- Type: Short answer
- Required: No
